About Bhagavad Gita

The Bhagavad Gita is a spiritual conversation between Lord Krishna and Arjuna, shared in the middle of a great battlefield. Arjuna feels lost and confused, and Krishna guides him with deep wisdom about life, duty, and inner strength.

The Gita teaches us how to stay calm during tough times, make the right choices, and stay connected to our true self. It shows different paths like action, devotion, and knowledge — helping everyone in their own way.

Even today, people read the Gita for peace, clarity, and inspiration. Its words remind us that we can face any situation with courage and faith. It is not just a book — it is a guide to living a meaningful and balanced life.
